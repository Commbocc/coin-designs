Communications & Digital Media Services

Communications and Digital Media Services works to tell the story of County government to our citizens. We incorporate the service objectives of customer service, transparency in government and promotions/public information throughout all of our work in order to provide citizens with a satisfying experience with their government. This department serves as an "in-house agency" for all other departments by formulating communications plans, preparing and disseminating key messages and promoting the County's brand.
